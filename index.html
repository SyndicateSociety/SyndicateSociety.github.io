Syndicate Society

Welcome home, pilot! Here you can breathe, log in, and fly EVE the way it’s meant to be played.

Syndicate Society (SYN.S) is a private EVE Online corporation built for players who enjoy a more casual approach to the game—whether that’s relaxed PVE or small-gang PVP.

New recruit pilots please check in-game Corp bulletin for top projects, guides and more.

For more than 15 years, the syndicate continues to thrive as a home and a forge for independent, whiskey-loving and great capsuleers.

In Syndicate Society, EVE is a hobby, not a second job. Within SYN.S, you’ll find a community of amazing pilots who value a laid-back atmosphere while still growing together in our highsec, lowsec and nullsec headquarters.

Pilot, Syndicate is your bastion, a place to gather strength, a home to become you.

We welcome players with busy lives outside the game—people who can’t commit to EVE full-time, but still want to explore its vast universe and steadily expand their horizons.

In Syndicate, learn the ultimate shortcuts of EVE Online that will drastically maximize your potentials in-game.
