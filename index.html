• After ~2 weeks, newbies learn practical EVE shortcuts through small projects (e.g. building a personal EVE spreadsheet alongside Syndicate’s Main Guide).

Casual gamers (adept level or bitter vets), especially soloists who enjoy flying alongside other solo-minded pilots.

International gamers who enjoy different cultures, time zones, and languages.

Pilots in PvX you blend PvE, PvP solo or small-party activity, and industry.

Syndicate welcomes the low-pressure gamers—the ones who get home after a long day of work and just want to log in to spin ships or relax. Pilot, Syndicate is your bastion—a place to gather strength and longevity, a home to become you.

Syndicate isn’t for you if

You expect instant content. Syndicate is a small, old-school corp. Many members balance careers, families, and real-life priorities.

You rely on others to generate content for you. While we do organize activities, this is not a 24/7 content farm. Pilots with strong initiative and (for new pilots) good “Google-fu” tend to thrive here.

You’re driven by power politics or constant warfare. While Syndicate has a soft-piracy and nullsec past, we value stability and long-term play over nonstop conflict.

We’re working professionals, common folk, supportive parents (dogs, fish, cows & cats included), yes—bachelors and bachelorettes, long-time small-party or solo pilots, and people who prefer calm over chaos. We share a love for sci-fi, and what truly makes Syndicate unique is welcoming pilots from all over the world—making us a truly international corp.

Prep Before Joining Syndicate

Before Joining you should have a basic foundation—EVE is a snail-paced game, and Syndicate values longevity. This means a high-sec base with backup ships and progress toward completing the AIR Career Missions and the Sisters of EVE / Fractured Legacy Epic Arcs. For new and adept pilots alike, solid core skills around cruiser level are expected, including Cloaking or equivalent hulls (Rifter, Astero, Thorax, or Drake). New pilots progress through T1 ships, while experienced pilots are expected—or willing to learn—to FC small-party PvX fleets, working toward two bases of operation in different regions. Many pilots burn out in EVE, most newbies quit—but for those who do, the journey is rewarding.

The Direction: Members deserve to log in, play in peace, and grow steadily through shared guides, utilities, and resources built for longevity.

What We Stand For: EVE Online is a hobby, not a race—grounded in balance, longevity, and real life first. By playing well in life, we play better in-game. If Syndicate feels like the right fit for you: join the in-game channel SYN.S and apply.
